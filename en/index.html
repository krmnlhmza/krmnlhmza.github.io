Frequently Asked Questions
How does the working process go?

The process starts with a needs analysis; once the scope is clear, a proposal is submitted, and development begins after approval. See the "How We Work" section for details.

Is integration with our existing systems possible?

Yes. Solutions are designed to integrate with the software and hardware infrastructure you already use.

Which industries do you work with?

The solutions are industry-independent; projects have been delivered in mining, human resources, food, and finance so far.

How is pricing determined?

Pricing is determined after the needs analysis, based on the project's scope and duration; a tailored quote is provided for each project.

Is support provided after delivery?

Yes. Maintenance, bug fixing, and further development support is provided after delivery.
